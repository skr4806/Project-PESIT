About

It is natural that many examinations are being held to grade students. One important aspect of these exams is the seating arrangement to prevent malpractice. Now, how hard could it be to plan for an arrangement? Well, it's complexity grows rapidly with the growth in the constraints viz. Number of types of classrooms (for eg. Seminar hall, our classroom, etc.), Number of classrooms of each type, number of students each type of bench could occupy (we introduce a concept of split here), number of students of each stream, etc. etc. Our project aims at providing options to input a collection of these constraints and then be able to design & return an appropriate seating arrangement keeping in mind the above constraints.
